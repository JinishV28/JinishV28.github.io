Community Service




Rotaract clubs bring together people ages 18 and older to exchange ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service.Community Service is the opportunity Rotaract Club of Fr.CRCE have to implement club projects and activities that improve life in the local community.

Flagship Events

Every year Rotaract Club of FR.CRCE conducts some of their flagship events. These are included in the club service events of the Club. These events are the highlighted events of the Rotaract Club cause they are done with the intention to gather more number of masses, to increase the brotherhood amongst them and spread love in as much quantity as possible.
