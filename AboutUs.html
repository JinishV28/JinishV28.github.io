Who We Are

Our Background

We are an integral part of the Rotaract which is a worldwide organization of young men and women aged 15 to 30 based in over 176 countries. The Rotaract Club of FR.CRCE comes under Zone 1A and district 3141.

What we do ?


  • We are a non-profit organisation committed to unlocking the highest potential of our members and have been leading change in various spheres.
  • We identify, process, and understand the problems that directly impact our lives and communities.
  • We believe that the youth is uniquely positioned to build more than just an inclusive world.
